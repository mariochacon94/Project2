THC

The Huntington Club was established in 2021. Formerly known as Seacliff Country Club, the world-renowned Travis Matthews purchased the golf course. At the age of 16, Travis Matthews worked in the pro shop as a cart boy, where he started designing golf t-shirts, ultimately creating a global fashion empire. The Huntington Club differentiates itself from surrounding clubs by fostering a beach community mentality. We focus on providing the best for our members and creating lasting memories.
